Gemeinsam fur die Gesundheit.
Integrity. Safety. Health.

The Bund philippinischer Gesundheitskräfte in Österreich (BPGÖ) is a professional organization dedicated to supporting and empowering Filipino healthcare workers in Austria. We promote collaboration, provide resources for career advancement, and advocate for the integration of Filipino professionals into the Austrian healthcare system, ensuring their success and well-being.

Past Events

BUND 1st Congress: Health Worker’s Day

The BUND 1st Congress: Health Worker’s Day was an inspiring and impactful gathering dedicated to honoring the dedication and resilience of health workers. This landmark event brought together healthcare professionals, policy-makers, and community leaders to celebrate the vital role health workers play in our society, especially in light of recent global challenges.

BARRIO Fiesta BUND First Aid

The Barrio Fiesta Bund First Aid was a vibrant celebration of Filipino culture, bringing together the community for a day filled with music, food, and fun. One of the key features of the event was the First Aid Station, which played a vital role in ensuring the safety and well-being of all attendees.
