Insights

"Welcome to the Bund Philippinischer Gesundheitskräfte in Österreich (BPGÖ). As the president of this vibrant organization, it is both an honor and a privilege to lead a community of passionate and dedicated Filipino healthcare professionals in Austria.

At BPGÖ, we believe in the power of unity, growth, and service. Our mission is to provide a platform that not only fosters professional excellence but also strengthens our cultural heritage and solidarity. Through collaboration and networking, we aim to support each member’s journey in healthcare, empowering you to reach new heights in your career while contributing meaningfully to both Filipino and Austrian communities.

As we continue to grow, our vision remains clear: to inspire, support, and uplift one another. Together, we can make a lasting impact in the healthcare sector and beyond, serving as role models for future generations of Filipino health professionals. I invite you to actively participate in our initiatives and make the most of the opportunities we offer.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Together, we will continue to thrive and excel."

- Marjorie Akistoy (President)

"Greetings and welcome to the Bund Philippinischer Gesundheitskräfte in Österreich (BPGÖ).

As Vice President, I am proud to support our incredible network of Filipino healthcare professionals here in Austria. Our organization thrives because of the collective passion, expertise, and dedication of each of you. At BPGÖ, we are committed to empowering our members through opportunities for professional development, mentorship, and community engagement.

In addition to advancing our careers, BPGÖ provides a space where we celebrate our shared Filipino heritage, promote cultural exchange, and contribute to the well-being of both our Filipino and Austrian communities. Together, we not only strengthen our professional ties but also nurture a sense of belonging that transcends borders.

As we move forward, I encourage each member to take advantage of the resources, events, and programs we offer. Your active participation is the key to building a stronger, more connected community. Let’s continue working hand-in-hand to achieve excellence, both in our individual careers and as a unified organization.

I am excited for what the future holds and look forward to seeing the positive impact we can make together."

- Daniel Garces (Vice President)
