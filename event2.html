BARRIO FIESTA BUND FIRST AID

The Barrio Fiesta Bund first aid was a vibrant celebration of Filipino culture, bringing together the community for a day filled with music, food, and fun. One of the key features of the event was the First Aid Station, which played a vital role in ensuring the safety and well-being of all attendees.
